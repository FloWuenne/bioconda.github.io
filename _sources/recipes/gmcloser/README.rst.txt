gmcloser
========

.. conda:recipe:: gmcloser
   :replaces_section_title:

   GMcloser fills and closes the gaps present in scaffold assemblies\, especially those generated by the de novo assembly of whole genomes with next\-generation sequencing \(NGS\) reads.

   :homepage: https://sourceforge.net/projects/gmcloser/
   :license: GPL / GPL v3
   :recipe: /`gmcloser <https://github.com/bioconda/bioconda-recipes/tree/master/recipes/gmcloser>`_/`meta.yaml <https://github.com/bioconda/bioconda-recipes/tree/master/recipes/gmcloser/meta.yaml>`_
